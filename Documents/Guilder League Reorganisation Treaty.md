
Article 1. 
The [[Guilder League]] will acknowledge that it illegally and woefully bribed the emperor of the empire in an effort to circumvent the [[Elven Settlement Treaty]]. 

Article 2. 
A permanent Embassy of the [[Guilder League]] will be established in Roseria's Fortress District.

Article 3. 
The [[Guilder League]] will pay reparations of 1 million Guilders in gold with interest and interest on interest for the lose of life, prosperity and property in Vorn. The exact interest rate will be determined by the [[vornari]] government upon activation of the treaty.

Article 4.
[[Guilder League]] traders in the Kingdom of [[Vorn]] are required to sell their goods for 7 days in Port Rosen. In continuation of the current trade customs of the city.

Article 5. Port Rosen shall be a member of the [[Guilder League]] again.

Article 6. The Free City of [[Hamburrow]] will give the leadership of the [[Guilder League]] to Port Rosen.

Article 7. The Free City of [[Hamburrow]] will give up the countryside territories it conquered in violation of imperial law and make proper reparations as agreed to by the to be compensated nations.

Article 8. The Free City of [[Hamburrow]] will transfer its illegally acquired electoral Status to the elven duchy of[[Silkenglade]] in accordance with the [[Elven Settlement Treaty]].

Article 9. Any failure to comply to any of the previous conditions will lead to the immediate reinstatement of the [[Guilder League Prohibition Act]].
