Excerpt of the Prima Bulla Imperiala (First Imperial Bull)
In recognition of the disappearance of our beloved and sacred emperor the imperial diet has decided on the intiative of the Grand Duchy of Vorn to introduce the following motion. The imperial throne should in absence of any advice for hereditary inheritance be elected. For undertakement of such glorious cause as electors have been chosen the following realms:
1. The Grand Duchy of Astviel, who shall also hold the title of Archmarshall, to ensure the need of the defensive and military of the empire. 
2. The Grand Duchy of Vorn, who shall also hold the title of Archsteward, to ensure the need of a proper bureaucracy of the empire. 
3. The Grand Duchy of Watar, who shall also hold the title of Archcup-bearer, to ensure the need of the less populus countries in the Empire.
4. The Grand Duchy of Viola, who shall also hold the title Archchamberlain, to ensure the need of the economics of the empire.
5. The Magocracy of the Towers, who shall also hold the title of Archmagos, to ensure the need of the magical in the empire. 
6. and 7. Two theocratic realms as decided by his Holyness. Each of which will hold the archchancellorship over one half of the empire in deputization of the emperor. 

Furthermore the imperial diet has decided to bar the following realms from an electoral position perpetually. 
1. The Archduchy of Veshni who shall be elected first non hereditary emperor.
2. The realm of his Holyness who shall crown the emperors.
