An omnibox is a game box with various easy to carry around games inside. Contents feature a set of various dice, multipurpose cards, small figurines, tokens and sticks as well as a set of rules printed in the most common local language of the area. The box itself can be unfolded to reveal a board plan on both sides. This easy to carry around item is famous among travellers and adventurers of all kinds and was originally developed in the imperial capital of Gametown by  Oziap Inc.. Nowadays it is distributed all over the known world and resold by various licensee partners of Oziap Inc..
