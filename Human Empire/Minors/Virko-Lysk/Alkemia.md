The Alkemia is one if not the foremost institutions of alchemy, [[Corpomorphosis]], and other related practices in the entire Empire. It's Alchemists are famous brewers, mutagenists and chirurgeons. Their services are sought after by many princes even far outside the Empire. Entry into the Alkemia is hard and bar a hefty stipend the Alkemia hands out for particularly talented students also just as costly.  Especially their corpomorphers are hugely desired in some parts of the empire. Thus leading to the Alkemia opening its one and only branch office in Nikoleapolis, capital of [[Lothvryn]]. It's history predates the empire by several centuries. Originally just one of many gnomish alchemical labs, the Alkemia evolved into a proper alchemical college in the wake of the fall of the [[Gnomium]]. It's remote location deep inside the swamps of [[Virko-Lysk]] may have aided it in this particular endeavour.
