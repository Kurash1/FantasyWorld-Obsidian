An ancient institution that dates back to the times of the ancient [[Gnomium]] these towers are the prime address for any academic study on magic. Most of these towers however have been lost during the Age of Collapse. Nowadays only 12 such towers are known to remain in operation. Originally these towers focused exclusively on their one portfolio excluding all others. But nowadays you can learn any subject at any tower academy. Students are free to use the teleportation circles between towers as long as it is for official curricular or extracurricular needs. 

---
[The Towers#Alder Tower]
Alder Tower:
A tower that focuses on abjuration, psychic powers, oracles, and conjuration. It is the inventor of the schools of gates and the boundary respectively. 

[The Towers#Ash Tower]
Ash Tower:
This tower is the inventor of the School of Ars Grammatica and focuses on Divination as well as Time and Rune Magic

[The Towers#Elder Tower]
Elder Tower:
Situated in [[Astviel]] this tower is a very esoteric and occult institution. It also heavily dabbles in necromancy much to the suspicion of the other academies. However if you need to find occult knowledge this is one of the best addresses for it. 

[The Towers#Hazel Tower]
Hazel Tower:
Not an inventor of any known major school per se this tower is heavily involved in the theoretical aspects of magic. However it also focuses on Magical Scripture itself and an obscure field called Magical Fiction. 

[The Towers#Holly Tower]
Holly Tower:
The most brutal war mages and evokers graduate from this academy. Countless generations of imperial and gnomish battle mages call this academy their alma mater. It invented the School of Battle Magic to nobody's surprise.

[The Towers#Juniper Tower]
Juniper Tower:
Wards and Spirits are the speciality of this rather esoteric Tower. Otherwise due to exactly those wards not much is known about this tower. 

[The Towers#Mistletoe Tower]
Mistletoe Tower:
The School of Rooted Wisdom has its place of origin here and the tower is deep into research about both Fate and Ritual.

[The Towers#Oak Tower]
Oak Tower:
The capital and elector country of the academy network. Situated in Lake Arcanis it stands as the proud center of the academic study of magic. Focusing hard on Magical Oversight of the other academies and magic in general. It naturally evolved into pursuing both order and judicial magic and invented the School of Civic Wizardry.

[The Towers#Rowan Tower]
Rowan Tower:
Originally destroyed during the Age of Collapse the Grand Duchy of [[Vorn]] agreed to rebuild the place in return for an eternal commitment to educate their [[Rose-Blessed]] sorcerers. Its original focus however is on fae magic, enchantment and illusions. It invented the School of Mentalism.

[The Towers#Willow Tower]
Willow Tower:
The common stereotype about this Tower is that all their students are lazy because they sleep a lot. Which is a gross oversimplification how else would one perform dream magic. And besides they also are heavily involved in alchemy, and the study of the stars as well. Henceforth sleep at day be awake at night is a thing here. Student complaints are very known to exist here because of the weird schedule.

[The Towers#Yew Tower]
Yew Tower:
A kind of magic focused solely on retaining vitality and shape. This Tower heavily aided the Myrkky Coven in the purification of [[Anasthasia Astiviella]] and also helped in the creation of [[Corpomorphosis]]. Its focus on Vitamancy and Transmutation led it to develop the School of Protean Form.
