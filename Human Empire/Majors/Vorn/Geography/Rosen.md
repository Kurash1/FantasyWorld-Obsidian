The Rosen is the mightiest River in [[Vorn]] and its valley is the most fertile land in all of the realm. It is here that most of the [[Halfling]] population of [[Vorn]] resides in. The flooded fertile valley not only makes the country self sufficient in terms of food but it also provides a vital water way, especially so at the mouth of the River at Port Rosen. In ancient times the demonic host was only stopped here after the mighty [[Thornkeep]] fell after a century of near constant siege. It is for all those reasons that the mighty Rosen is nowadays viewed, rightfully so, as the lifeblood of [[Vorn]].
